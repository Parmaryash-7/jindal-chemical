Jindal Group is a diverse business conglomerate, engaged in Textile, Packaging, Speciality Chemicals and Mobilitric (EV) segment. Group has a rich legacy of 3 decades in the leading position in many business areas.

We embarked on this journey in the early 80s and have continued to grow strongly, underpinning our innovation capabilities and state-of-the-art manufacturing unit that brings in the flavour of diversity in our fabric manufacturing facility.

Jindal Speciality Chemicals is a part of the Jindal Group. It is conceived with a vision of “Become a leading speciality chemical company by delivering best-in-class products that enrich the quality of life”. The company has identified speciality chemicals as its springboard for growth, aligned with the country’s vision of “Aatmanirbhar Bharat”. Over the last many years, the company has invested heavily in its R&D and innovation and is now well-placed to be part of this growing segment. Our subsidiary Expede-Tech Research & Development is responsible for innovation, experimentation, problem-solving and development of new, safer and sustainable production processes.
