ACETONITRILE

Acetonitrile, is the simplest organic nitrile with the formula CH3CN and structure H3C-C≡N. It appears as a colorless liquid with an aromatic odor. It is mainly used as a polar aprotic solvent in organic synthesis and in the purification process. It is also used to make pharmaceuticals, perfumes, rubber products, pesticides, acrylic nail removers and batteries.

Technology

Fixed-bed vapour phase reaction and fractional distillation.

Chemistry

Catalytic ammonolysis of acetic acid.

Applications

Laboratory Chemical: Mobile phase in HPLC.
Synthesis Intermediate: Perfumes, Agro and Pharmaceutical industry.
Solvent: Extraction, Electrochemical cells, Pharmaceutical, Biochemical synthesis process.
Other Industrial Application: Removing solution, transition metal complex catalyst
